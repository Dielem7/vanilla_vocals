Singing Lesons

What to expect

The first time visiting Vanilla Vocals will be different than your following lessons. A vocal evaluation will determine your range, vocal capabilities and voice type.

Your vocal evaluation will be 30 minutes and you will also have the opportunity to meet your coach and talk to her about what your goals are.

Lessons are at least once a week for thirty, forty-five or sixty minutes. During lessons there will be focused on breathing, vocal technique and vocal fitness.

Depending on the challenges you face as a singer, lessons will be tailored to suit you. The coach will also help you to choose an appropriate song for your voice type.

Working towards a common goal

Vanilla Vocals’ students perform at many competitions and concerts throughout the year. If this is something you would like to do, you and your couch will work on four songs in different genres.

It doesn't matter how big or small your goal is but it is important to set goals for yourself, it can be to broaden your vocal range, learn a certain song, record your own song or a cover song, or even just to let go a little and have fun.

Talk to you coach about your goals.

Piano Lessons

Vanilla Vocals also offers Piano lessons for beginners up to piano level 3. Piano Lessons also includes music theory.

Ukulele Lessons

We like to encourage our vocal students to learn at least one other instrument. The Ukulele is a very easy to learn and a wonderful instrument to use when writing your own songs. This is also a great instrument for children interested in guitar who’s hands are still a bit too small. Ukulele lessons are only for beginners.

Recordings And Youtube Covers

Vanilla Vocals’ Recording studio is ideal for voiceovers, or students who want to record their own songs, or cover songs. We also have equipment and software to make high quality YouTube cover songs.

ProTools

For those who are considering a career in music or purely have a passion to record and process their own recordings, modify pitch, write CD’s, changing formats of songs and edit songs, this course may be a valuable first step to spread your wings. Parents are also welcome to run the course. Your teacher will assist you in downloading a free basic version of Protools to get you going. This can be done using your own PC if the specifications are compatible or on the studio’s PC and sound equipment.

Gigs

Singing coach Lize-Marié Smit will often take her best students along with her on gigs. Hire Vanilla Vocals to come and perform at your wedding, party or corporate event. We have different types of singers for different types of occasions.
